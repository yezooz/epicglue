About this project

Warrior Clash is a capture and elimination strategy game that pits greatest warriors in history against each other. 

For casual gamers and families alike!

Players draft teams of warriors, defend their land, gather resources and develop strategies to conquer their enemies. These battles are played atop modular game boards which create hundreds of possible maps.

Each warrior has a different arrangement of trait scores between the categories of Grit, Agility, Ingenuity and Strength.

When you back, you just aren't going to get an awesome game, but you will be launching a small business of passionate creators dedicated to making top notch gateway & family games for years to come.

 

 

As warriors are moved throughout the game board, the players will attempt to rely on relative strengths and exploit weaknesses of their enemies. When matching sides of two opponent warriors are aligned, they can do battle. The trait scores determine the handicap of the fight, but rolls of the dice determine the victor.

Will your team be the last one standing?

In addition to crazy battle fun, Warrior Clash intends to spark cultural curiosity and show women in dynamic, exciting roles.

Clint Clark is the co-designer and founder of Clark Creative. With a dozen of years of designing games and digital content along with one successful Kickstarter under his belt, he's excited to bring more family fun to your tabletop. 

Emmanuel Sylvester is the lone artist on the project and has brought the warriors to life. We believe in having single artists on game projects so the look is seamless and the universe feels united.

MacKenzie Clark is the cornerstone of Clark Creative. She plays a significant role in game testing and product development. Outside of the game universe she leads the creation efforts of educational products inspired by the Clark Creative game universes. 

 

 

 

 

 

Risks and challenges

We're ready to go.

Having already completed a game project through Kickstarter (under an individual account, shifted to company account) we are well prepared for the obstacles that may come our way.

To mitigate issues, we've partnered with companies that have delivered on many successful Kickstarter projects. WinGo is a widely respected manufacturer that creates many, many terrific Kickstarter projects. We've also partnered with Ship Naked to fulfill the orders to keep the costs as low as possible. Ship Naked has successfully completed hundreds of projects.

This doesn't mean that we won't have any issue, but we think partnering with top notch folks in their field limits the possibilities. Through the other companies we've started we've completed hundreds of thousands of transactions and always ensure customer satisfaction.

No different here, if you are not satisfied there is a money-back guarantee.
